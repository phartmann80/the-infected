# Environment Bible

Status: Draft framework, no canonical environment approved yet.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

## Creation rule

Start with:

```text
Environment 001
```

Environment 001 must answer:

> What does The Infected look like?

Once Environment 001 is approved, future locations inherit its visual DNA.

## Environment 001 required qualities

Environment 001 should include:

- ruined urban street
- abandoned vehicles
- empty checkpoint or barricade
- emergency lighting
- drifting smoke
- burning debris
- damaged infrastructure
- traces of evacuation
- survivor presence
- distant infected movement or implied threat

## Environmental storytelling rule

Every visible object should raise a question.

Examples:

- Why is that car abandoned?
- Who built this barricade?
- Why is the checkpoint empty?
- What caused the fire?
- Why is the radio still active?
- Who left that bag behind?

## Visual DNA

Environment 001 should establish:

- material language
- lighting language
- density of props
- level of destruction
- color grading
- fog/smoke behavior
- emergency/military presence
- readable focal points for mobile

## Canonical gate

An environment becomes canonical only after:

- art direction approval
- story logic review
- Android feasibility review
- asset provenance recorded
- registry status updated from `internal-review` to `approved` or `canonical`
