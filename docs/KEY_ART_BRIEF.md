# Key Art Brief

Status: Draft brief.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

## Objective

Create one official hero composition worthy of:

- website hero
- Google Play feature graphic direction
- press kit
- trailer thumbnail
- Steam-style capsule inspiration
- social announcement art

This should not be a generic background. It should become the visual identity anchor for The Infected.

## Required composition qualities

The key art should communicate:

- ruined city
- survivor presence
- infected threat
- abandoned checkpoint or vehicle
- firelight and smoke
- cold blue shadows
- mystery and danger
- hope within collapse

## Focal hierarchy

1. Emotional scene read: something terrible happened here.
2. Survivor/infected tension.
3. The world/environment.
4. Logo integration.
5. Platform/CTA variants as needed.

## Asset discipline

Do not create final key art until Survivor 001, Infected 001, and Environment 001 are approved or explicitly allowed for internal-review use.

Temporary key art may be created as `internal-review`, `canonical: false`, `temporary: true`.

## Cropping requirements

Future key art should support:

- wide website hero
- square social crop
- vertical mobile crop
- Google Play feature crop
- trailer thumbnail crop

Design the composition so it survives cropping without losing the story.
