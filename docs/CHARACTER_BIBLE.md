# Character Bible

Status: Draft framework, no canonical characters approved yet.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

## Creation rule

Do not generate dozens of characters.

Start with:

```text
Survivor 001
```

Survivor 001 should establish the visual language for every future survivor.

## Survivor 001 design goals

Survivor 001 should feel:

- capable but vulnerable
- practical, not superheroic
- emotionally worn
- dressed for survival, not fashion
- readable in silhouette
- believable inside Environment 001
- aligned with future Android gameplay readability

## Approval questions

Before Survivor 001 becomes canonical, answer:

1. What did this person do before the outbreak?
2. Why are they still alive?
3. What have they lost?
4. What visible equipment tells their story?
5. How do they move?
6. What is recognizable about their silhouette?
7. Can the design read at mobile scale?

## Prohibited for now

- large character rosters
- hero-team lineups
- final names without lore approval
- costume variants
- faction uniforms without faction approval
- unapproved backstory dumps

## Canonical gate

A character becomes canonical only after:

- concept approval
- provenance recorded
- visual design approved
- story role approved
- asset registry status updated from `internal-review` to `approved` or `canonical`
