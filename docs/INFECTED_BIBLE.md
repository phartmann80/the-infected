# Infected Bible

Status: Draft framework, no canonical infected design approved yet.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

## Creation rule

Do not generate enemy rosters yet.

Start with:

```text
Infected 001
```

Infected 001 should establish the visual and behavioral threat language for the game.

## Infected 001 design goals

Infected 001 should feel:

- human enough to be disturbing
- physically dangerous
- not a cartoon zombie
- readable in silhouette
- plausible in the world
- behaviorally unpredictable
- usable in future Android gameplay

## Threat philosophy

The infected should create tension before direct contact.

Use:

- distant movement
- partial silhouettes
- sound before sight
- broken posture
- speed implied by framing
- unnatural stillness

Avoid relying only on gore.

## Approval questions

Before Infected 001 becomes canonical, answer:

1. What makes this infected visually distinct?
2. How does it move?
3. What warning does the player get before danger?
4. What emotion should it create: fear, disgust, panic, dread, or uncertainty?
5. How does it read in low light?
6. How does it read on Android?
7. What gameplay behavior does the design imply?

## Canonical gate

An infected design becomes canonical only after:

- visual concept approval
- behavior philosophy approval
- gameplay readability review
- provenance recorded
- asset registry status updated from `internal-review` to `approved` or `canonical`
