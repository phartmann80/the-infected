# ADR-005 Audio Direction

## Status

Draft

## Approval Date

Pending

## Decision

The audio direction will use silence, distant environmental cues, failing infrastructure, and sparse musical tension rather than constant soundtrack coverage.

## Reasoning

The city should feel alive after collapse. Subtle sound layers should make the player believe danger exists beyond the frame.

## Alternatives Considered

- Continuous horror music.
- Frequent jump-scare stingers.
- Dense audio beds that fill every second.

These alternatives reduce tension and make the world feel less believable.

## Consequences

Future sound assets should prioritize environmental storytelling, restraint, and evolving ambience. Public narration must use controlled prerecorded assets, not browser-generated voices.
