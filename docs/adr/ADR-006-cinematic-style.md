# ADR-006 Cinematic Style

## Status

Draft

## Approval Date

Pending

## Decision

The cinematic style will use deliberate film language: establish, reveal, build tension, surprise, and transition. Camera movement must have narrative purpose.

## Reasoning

The landing experience should feel like a trailer sequence, not a website animation. Every shot should guide attention and deepen curiosity.

## Alternatives Considered

- Random flythrough camera motion.
- Fast trailer chaos in the first hero.
- Decorative motion unrelated to story.

These alternatives reduce emotional clarity and make the experience feel generic.

## Consequences

Future cinematics should be planned shot-by-shot with clear purpose before generation or production begins.
