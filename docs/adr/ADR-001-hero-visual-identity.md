# ADR-001 Hero Visual Identity

## Status

Draft

## Approval Date

Pending

## Decision

The hero visual identity should center on a ruined city cinematic frame: abandoned vehicles, emergency lighting, smoke, ash, fire against cold blue shadows, survivor presence, and distant infected movement.

## Reasoning

This direction supports [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](../CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md) by making the environment carry the emotional question: "What happened here?"

## Alternatives Considered

- Character-only hero poster.
- Logo-focused teaser with abstract smoke.
- Gameplay UI-first presentation.

These are less effective because they do not establish the world as the primary emotional hook.

## Consequences

Future hero assets must prioritize environmental storytelling, cinematic lighting, and clear mobile-readable focal points before adding secondary page sections.
