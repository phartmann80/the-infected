# ADR-003 Infected 001

## Status

Draft

## Approval Date

Pending

## Decision

Infected 001 will be the first infected concept and must establish the threat language before additional enemy classes are generated.

## Reasoning

The infected should feel human enough to disturb, dangerous enough to drive survival decisions, and readable enough for Android gameplay.

## Alternatives Considered

- Generate many infected variants immediately.
- Use exaggerated monster designs.
- Lead with gore instead of tension.

These alternatives risk losing the grounded survival identity defined in [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](../CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md).

## Consequences

Future infected classes must inherit visual and behavioral DNA from Infected 001 unless a later accepted ADR supersedes this decision.
