# Visual Reference Board

Status: Qualitative reference board, no copied artwork.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

This is not a mood board of pasted images. It documents the qualities The Infected should pursue. Public references may be cited for discussion, but copyrighted artwork should not be embedded or copied into the repository.

## Lighting

Desired qualities:

- heavy atmosphere
- shafts of light through smoke
- practical fire lighting
- emergency red/amber lights
- overcast skies
- cold blue shadow fill
- headlights through fog
- partial silhouette reveals

Reference qualities, not copies:

- The Last of Us marketing: grounded emotional lighting and environmental sadness.
- Dead Space remake: practical light sources and oppressive darkness.
- Resident Evil Village: controlled reveal and gothic survival mood.
- Dying Light / Days Gone: outdoor survival danger, abandoned infrastructure, infected scale.

## Architecture

Desired qualities:

- abandoned urban streets
- military checkpoints
- collapsed infrastructure
- boarded storefronts
- damaged overpasses
- evacuation barriers
- temporary quarantine structures
- burned-out vehicles
- emergency signage
- broken power systems

The city should feel lived-in before collapse, not invented only for combat.

## Camera

Desired qualities:

- slow push-ins
- wide establishing shots
- low street-level frames
- shoulder-level tension
- foreground obstruction
- smoke reveal
- held frames that let the viewer search for detail
- restrained camera breathing

Avoid random flythroughs, constant orbiting, or motion without story purpose.

## Emotion

Desired qualities:

- isolation
- desperation
- determination
- fear without cheap shock
- curiosity before explanation
- hope within collapse
- survival tension

The viewer should feel there is a story behind every object.

## Texture and atmosphere

Desired qualities:

- wet asphalt
- ash
- smoke
- dust
- rust
- cracked glass
- emergency tape
- burnt paint
- dirty cloth
- worn metal
- faint film grain
- subtle chromatic aberration
- restrained bloom

Effects should support realism and mood, not announce themselves.

## Android practicality

Every reference quality should be translated into practical game language:

- readable silhouettes
- controllable fog density
- optimized particles
- baked lighting where possible
- restrained post-processing
- mobile-safe contrast
- scalable asset detail
