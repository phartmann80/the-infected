# Chapter Structure

Status: Draft landing-page story structure.

Primary reference: [CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md](./CREATIVE_DIRECTION.md)

## Philosophy

Do not think in website sections.

Think in cinematic chapters.

The visitor should feel like they are descending into the world, not reading a brochure.

## Approved chapter direction

### 1. Arrival

The first frame. Isolation, silence, smoke, and unanswered questions.

### 2. The Outbreak

Show traces of the collapse, not an exposition dump.

### 3. Survivors

Introduce human stakes through Survivor 001 and survival choices.

### 4. The Infected

Introduce danger through Infected 001 and behavior, not gore alone.

### 5. Arsenal

Weapons and equipment should feel improvised, scarce, and consequential.

### 6. The Mission

Clarify the survival objective and why the player keeps moving.

### 7. Join the Survivors

The conversion moment. The visitor should already want in before the CTA appears.

## Transition rule

Scrolling should feel like moving deeper into the city.

Use:

- smoke/fog transitions
- sound evolution
- camera descent
- lighting changes
- chapter reveals motivated by environment

Avoid abrupt brochure-like section cuts.
